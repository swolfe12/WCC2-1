Welcome to WCC!

We’re thrilled you’ve found us! At WCC, we’re a family of believers living in the freedom and light of Christ, and we’re excited to share this journey with you.

Here’s what we’re all about:

Transformed Lives: We’ve been radically changed by God’s grace.
Freedom from the Treadmill: We’ve stepped off the “church treadmill” to embrace what Jesus has done for us.
Accepted and Loved: We’re learning to live fully in the love and acceptance God has for us.
Living Free and Light: We’re experiencing the true freedom that comes from knowing Jesus.
Join us in this incredible journey!
